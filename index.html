I am a senior research associate in the School of Mathematics at the University of East Anglia. From 2017 to 2019 I was a postdoctoral associate at Carnegie Mellon University and concurrently a junior researcher at the National Academy of Sciences of Armenia. I did my PhD (DPhil) at the University of Oxford under the supervision of Boris Zilber and Jonathan Pila. I received my bachelor's degree from Yerevan State University in 2013.
